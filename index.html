Negotiate at Site Visit

💰

23% Service Fee

Above industry norm (18–20%). Even 2–3% off saves $300–$500 on the bill.

🍹

Bartender Fee ($225)

On top of a 23% service charge this is double-dipping. Ask for it absorbed into the package.

🥗

Custom Hors d'Oeuvres

Ask to mix and match between Silver and Gold menus. Low cost to them, big win for you.

🛏️

Comp Suite + Resort Fee Waiver

Standard perk when you're paying 15–20 rooms. Resort fee waiver saves $1,200–$1,800 across all rooms.

Tips & Best Practices

Puerto Rico Legal: Blood tests must be done within 10 days of the ceremony. License fee is $250, paid to the PR Demographic Registry.

Catering

📋

Bring Competing Quotes

Get a written quote from Wyndham Grand Rio Mar before the site visit. El Con negotiates harder when they know you have options.

🍽️

Request a Tasting

Ask for a food tasting at the site visit or 45–60 days before. Don't commit to Filet Mignon at $309/person without tasting it.

📅

April = Peak Season

Lock in your contract ASAP — prices increase and availability tightens for prime April dates.

👥

Duo Entrée Option

+$15/person lets guests choose between two mains. For 30 guests that's $450 — removes pressure of a single pre-select.

Room Block

⚠️

Attrition Clause

If rooms go unused, you can owe 50–80% of the rate per empty room. Push for a courtesy block (no penalty) or 70% attrition threshold.

🗓️

Later Cutoff Date

Standard is 30 days. Push for 45–60 days — reduces financial risk from slow bookings.

🎁

Perks to Ask For

Comp suite, resort fee waiver, one free night per 10 rooms booked, and a hospitality suite for getting ready.

Destination Logistics

✈️

No Passport Needed

Puerto Rico is U.S. territory — no passport required for American guests. Big plus for attendance.

🌤️

April Weather

Generally dry, 80–85°F. Outdoor events are comfortable. Have a rain contingency in the contract.

🏖️

El Yunque Day Trip

20 min from El Con — great guest activity before or after the wedding. El Con concierge can arrange tours.

📸

Hire a Local Photographer

Local PR photographers know the light and locations — and cost less than mainland photographers with travel fees.

🤝

Use Local Vendors

Local florists, DJs, and bakers offer better value. El Con will provide a preferred vendor list at your site visit.
